CONSCIUS EXCLUSIVE A/W 2020

We’ve taken your leftovers and turned them into something elegant. This collection is crafted from sustainable materials made from waste.

Textile to tectule recycling

Materials and clothing that should have been discarded can now be transformed into poetic new styles made from recycled polyester from a closed circuit.

Wood pulp

The silk of the future is made of circular materials. Fiber pulp from sustainable sources and recycled plastic is used to create Naia ™ Renew.


Food crop waste

Where some see waste, we see opportunities for recycling. The Agraloop ™ process transforms oily hemp waste into new and natural Hemp Biofibers ™.

“For A/W20, we really wanted to be trailblazers – pushing the limits of creativity and sustainable fashion – by focusing on waste. As a result, the pieces in this collection are crafted from truly amazing materials produced from waste. Working with this kind of transformation and being able to speak to our customers through beauty, we hope that waste can be part of the future of sustainable fashion,” says Ann-Sofie Johansson, creative advisor at H&M.
