BRING COLOR BACK TO EARTH

GROW IT

You need clothing and we want you to have it. But not at any cost anymore. You have changed that. You and your generation have pushed us to look inwards and you demand that we change. For the better of the world. So we will try our best to live up to you. That is why we have come up with Grow It. A bag of seeds to be planted anywhere that become plants which increase biodiversity.

We want you on the journey with us. It’s very simple. Plant it, picture it, tag it, repeat. Fulfill missions and earn rewards. THANK YOU for changing us.

YOU ORDER, WE DONATE

YOU CAN BE THE HERO

Every hero needs a sidekick. Let us help you help us! For every 1000 #growit bags planted and tagged we donate 10.000 pounds to our sustainable research.

We have a vision. In 2030 we want our brand to be completely circular. In order to do that, we need to figure out how to make 100% of clothing waste in to new collections. We need to come up with new sustainable binding agents and rethink the use of fibres and textiles.

We make it. You wear it. Let’s change it together.
